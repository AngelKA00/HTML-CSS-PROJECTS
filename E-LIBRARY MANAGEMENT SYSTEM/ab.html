About us

Global E-Library (GEL) is a virtual repository of learning resources. It is sponsored and mentored by Ministry of Education, Government of Texas, through its Education Agency. GEL provides user group-specific services such as Novels and Technical resources. Services for Writers and general learners are also provided. GEL is designed to hold content of most common language i.e English. It is built to provide support for aspiring writers and life-long learners. It is designed to enable people to spend their time on a useful source. and gain skills in reading and writing. It is developed, operated and maintained by Government of Texas.
